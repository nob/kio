About US

We Japanese are very proud of our traditional folk style costume, the kimono. The "Kyo-Yuzen" dyeing process is a beautiful and quite a famous technique in Japan, as well as the lovely "Kyo-Shishu" Japanese style of embroidery.

Our vision is to help everyone personally experience and feel the beauty of Japanese culture by wearing one of our kimonos.

We create kimonos which are simple, clean cut reduced in style, with beautiful and unique designs.

We conduct every step in manufacturing process ourselves from creating the initial designs to packaging the items directly before shipment, so we are very proud of the very high quality of our products.

We are working daily to meet the high demand for our products not only domestically in the Japanese market, but internationally as well.

We passionately are working to create an entirely new value of Japanese style and clothing, while building broad awareness of the "Kyo-Yuzen" dyeing process and "Kyo-Shishu" embroidery style in the context of modern lifestyles across the world.
